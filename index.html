About Us

Blue Tide Chapel is a community centered around faith, committed to spreading the word of God and supporting those in need. We follow the teachings of Christ and stand by the Nicene-Constantinopolitan Creed of 381.

Our Mission

At Blue Tide Chapel, our mission is to nurture faith, spread the Gospel, and build a compassionate, faith-driven community. We believe in empowering individuals to live out the teachings of Jesus Christ through love, service, and grace.

Statement of Faith

We affirm the Nicene-Constantinopolitan Creed (381) as the foundation of our faith. We believe in the Holy Trinity, the Church's mission, and the promise of eternal life through Jesus Christ.
